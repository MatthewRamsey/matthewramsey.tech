readAR Project Instructions

  1. Download readAR.
  2. Use a dollar bill (USD) or download this picture and print/cut it out to use as the stage.
  3. Place the stage ($1 bill) on a flat, well lit surface.
  4. Open readAR and center the stage in the camera.
  5. Choose a story and enjoy the app!
  6. Tip: If the models disappear, try to re-center the stage in the camera. If that does not work, then remove the stage from the camera's view for a few seconds and then place it back. As a last resort, reload the scene by pressing the "Reload" button or restart the app.
